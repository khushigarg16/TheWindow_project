Work we do

At the Window Project, we work closely with self and systems and are committed to partnering with you to build high-impact solutions for your growth.


Every project we undertake is the Window Project for us.


It is an opportunity to immerse ourselves in your reality and work on solutions that emerge from different vantage points.

High-Performance Culture

How might we articulate and shape a high-performance culture?


Companies in India with strong strategy-culture alignment reported 2.4 times more profitability than companies with no strategy-culture alignment.


We help organizations articulate, shape and nurture the culture that takes their strategy forward.

Hiring Right

How might we ensure that high-quality candidates are hired?

Reports suggest that India is among the top four nations when it comes to companies making a wrong choice in hiring an employee.

A single bad hire can cost the company over INR 20 lakh.


We help start-ups and young organizations strengthen their hiring process. Our aim is to build internal capability to hire for knowledge, skills and attitude using objective methods.

Leadership Development

How might we multiply capabilities of next line of leaders?

Companies that invest in leadership development deliver stock market returns five times higher than the returns of companies that place less emphasis on human capital.


With you, we co-create learning experiences seeped in your context, by identifying real on-the-job projects. We also move beyond skill-building and work through mindsets that impede behavioral change.

Teaming-Up

How might we truly harness the power of working together?


A Global Survey showed that only 17% of Indian executives touted teamwork as their strength.


From teaming-up with cross-functional teams to working with top-teams in their vision and strategy, our solutions build a psychologically safe space to build trust and maximize impact.

Diversity & Inclusion

How might we foster innovation, creativity and empathy?

A BCG study found that companies with more diverse management teams have 19% higher revenues due to innovation.


We understand that promoting D&I in the workplace is a constant work in progress. Through appreciative inquiry, our solutions draw-out voices from diverse groups and encourages all to contribute fully.

Mindfulness & Resilience

How might we prevent employee burnout?

Research shows that employees experiencing burnout are 2.6 times as likely to be actively seeking for a job change, 63% more likely to take a sick day and half as likely to discuss performance goals with their managers.


Our solutions are geared to help participants internalize tangible tools to build resilience and deal with high-pressure.
